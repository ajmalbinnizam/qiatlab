About us

Institute of Automobile Technology is the pioneer in automobile electronic sector which provides quality training in automobile technology. We are the first and best automobile electronic institute in India. Our vision is not only impart technical education in automobile industry, but making them latest up-to-date and industry fit candidate to give best service of any vehicles with current technology.

Why Study at QIAT

The advancement of technology is much faster in the automotive industry than any other sectors.

Therefore, QIAT is one of the best institutions in South India for those who wish to study in this field, for employees currently working in the field who wish for upgradation, for those who have completed B. E, B. Tech and Diploma to get better placement and for those with Plus Two basic qualifications to find job in this field. Many of our alumni are doing excellent jobs outside India mainly in GCC countries. Those who have three to five years of work experience after the course are running their own institutions.
